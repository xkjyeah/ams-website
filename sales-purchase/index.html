Rental of Oxygen Cylinders

AMS offers rental of oxygen cylinders for home care. Our oxygen cylinder is made of aluminium alloy and comes with an attached flow regulator. It has a carrying handle for easy handling; it does not rust therefore it is not possible for the patient to inhale rust particles. Our cylinders come in the following sizes according to the volume of liquid oxygen within: 2.9L, 3.4L, 4.6L and 10.5L.

Sale of Automated External Defibrillator (AED)

AMS is the appointed sales agent for HeartSine Automated External Defibrillator (AED). The device comes with a 8-year warranty provided by HeartSine against device malfunction.

Each AED comes with:

  1. 1 lithium battery (non-rechargeable)
  2. 1 pair of adult defibrillation pads
  3. Optional AED cabinet — installation included

We also sell consumables such as the battery and a pair of adult defibrillation pads together.

We do not stock the paediatric defibrillation pads.
